Tingbo Hou

Currently, I am a senior staff software engineer at Google. I'm leading a small applied research team working on generative AI and computer vision. Anchored on efficiency, innovations, and applications, our work spans from traditional CV tasks, to GAN/StyleGAN, diffusion models, and text-to-image generation. The team delivered 10+ launches in major Google products, including Photos, YouTube, Meet, Ads, Cloud, Pixel, etc.


Prior to Google, I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stony Brook University, and B.S. from University of Science and Technology of China.
